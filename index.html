About

Where mission-critical meets modern technology.

I'm Rudi Mohamed — a public-sector CIO leading technology transformation where the stakes are real and the systems can't afford to fail. Today I oversee systems managing $7B+, after fifteen years in government IT and a tour at Microsoft.

I was honored as 2025 ORBIE CIO of the Year for work modernizing systems most leaders would rather leave alone — the high-consequence, highly regulated infrastructure the public depends on. I write about the leadership playbooks behind that work in The RockStar IT Leader Handbook and on my Substack, The Digital Edge.

My focus now is simple: help organizations modernize the systems that can't fail — and help the leaders responsible for them lead with clarity, confidence, and a clear technical edge.
